Objectives and Responsibilities

Objectives

  • Establish a university in the Maldives that delivers Islamic higher education.
  • Develop Maldivian citizens who are civilized, responsible, unified, and united.
  • Work within an Islamic perspective to reach a developed status for the citizens and the nation.
  • Advance and develop in Islamic science and other disciplines.
  • Develop creative and capable graduates to build the nation and the Islamic community.

Responsibilities

  1. Increasing and extending knowledge and skills in general, and specifically Islamic knowledge, research, and culture.
  2. Providing knowledge and skills in general, as well as higher education and training needed for the Maldives, in all areas of Islamic education.
  3. Conducting academic research and disseminating this research for the benefit of the Maldivian and international communities.
  4. Conferring Bachelor's, Master's, and Doctoral degrees and certificates, and other awards, for the training and educational programs of the University.
  5. Establishing relations with educational institutions in the Maldives and abroad for mutual benefit.
  6. Developing existing resources and acquiring further resources to achieve the aims of the University.
  7. Distributing the resources of the University fairly between all the centers of the University to achieve effectiveness.
  8. Developing and furthering Islamic and Maldivian history, traditions, and social mores.
  9. Carrying out any activity within the bounds of Islamic Shariah and Maldivian law to carry out the responsibilities above.
