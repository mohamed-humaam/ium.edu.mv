Vision & Mission

Vision of IUM

The ultimate aim of IUM is to set a new horizon for Islamic higher education to serve the people of the Maldives and the Ummah. The University intends to transform into a center of higher education excellence by upholding Article 10 of the Constitution of the Republic of Maldives which states that;

  • The religion of the state of the Maldives is Islam. Islam shall be the basis of all the laws of the Maldives.
  • No law contrary to any tenet of Islam shall be enacted in the Maldives.

Accordingly, the Vision of IUM is as follows:

Becoming the premier University advancing beneficial knowledge to the nation and Ummah

Mission of IUM

The mission statement defines the overall purpose of the University. The fundamental purpose of the existence of IUM is enshrined in the Islamic University of Maldives Act, ratified by HE President Abdulla Yamin Abdul Gayoom on 9th of July 2015.

IUM is mandated to achieve the following objectives through the aforementioned Act.

  1. Protect and preserve the Islamic civilization, culture, and education in all spheres of development in the Republic of Maldives within the overall perspective of religious harmony and national unity.
  2. Produce skillful and knowledgeable, mature, responsible graduates who uphold the unity of religion.
  3. Create a pool of talents necessary for national developments within the realm of the Islamic worldview.
  4. Develop and excel in key result areas of Islamic sciences and other branches in knowledge.
  5. Meet the needs of the nation and the Ummah for competent, innovative and well-rounded graduates having a balanced personality.

Accordingly, the Mission of IUM is:

To produce outstanding students with balanced personality to attain success in this life and in the eternal life hereafter, in alignment with the national, regional as well as the needs of the Ummah through excellence in teaching, learning, faculty research and scholarship and engagement in community service.

The key elements of this mission are;

  • Producing highly qualified career-oriented graduates with a balanced personality
  • Excellence in teaching, research, and innovation
  • Fostering research and innovation
  • Nurturing Islamic values and integration of knowledge
  • Service to the community
  • Instilling commitment to life-long learning
  • Internationalization
  • Dissemination of beneficial knowledge
