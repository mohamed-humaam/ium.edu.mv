Message From Dean

At IUM, we are committed to providing a supportive and enriching environment that fosters the holistic development of our students. We believe that education is not only about acquiring knowledge but also about personal growth, character development, and a sense of community.


Our dedicated team is here to assist and guide you throughout your academic journey. Whether you need academic support, counseling services, or have any concerns related to campus life, our doors are always open. We aim to create a safe and inclusive campus where every student can thrive and achieve their goals.


I encourage you to explore the various resources and opportunities available on our website to enhance your university experience. Feel free to reach out to our office for any assistance or to share your feedback.


As the Dean of Students, my primary goal is to ensure that your time at IUM is not only academically rewarding but also a time of personal growth and memorable experiences. I am here to listen to your ideas, address your concerns, and work together with the student body to make our university community even stronger.


I look forward to getting to know each of you and wish you all a successful and fulfilling journey here at the Islamic University of Maldives.


Warm regards,


Overview

Welcome to the Department of Student Affairs at the Islamic University of Maldives. We are here to support your academic and personal journey, providing services to help you succeed and thrive during your time at our university.


Our offerings include academic support, counseling, career guidance, and a focus on building a vibrant and inclusive campus community. We're dedicated to your growth and success.

Vision

The Department of Student Affairs at the Islamic University of Maldives is committed to enhancing the overall student experience by providing a range of services and programs that support students in their academic, personal, and social development. Our mission is to create a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student can thrive, grow, and achieve their full potential.

Mission

At the Islamic University of Maldives, we're dedicated to empowering students, fostering an inclusive community, and promoting personal and academic growth. As the Dean of Students, my mission is to lead with empathy and dedication, working collaboratively with our diverse student body to create a thriving university environment.
