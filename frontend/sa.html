Message From Registrar

Dear Esteemed Students,


I extend a warm and heartfelt welcome to the Islamic University of Maldives on behalf of the Office of the Registrar of Student Services. Our mission is to ensure that your academic journey is not only successful but also a transformative experience that prepares you for a bright and fulfilling future.


As the Registrar, I am here to assist you in various aspects of your university life. From admissions and course enrollment to maintaining your academic records, our team is dedicated to facilitating a seamless and enriching educational experience.


Our office is more than just a bureaucratic hub; it's a gateway to your dreams and aspirations. Whether you need guidance on academic planning, clarification on university policies, or assistance in navigating the various resources available to you, we are here to help. We take pride in upholding the integrity and accuracy of your academic records, ensuring that your hard work and accomplishments are duly recognized.


We understand that each student is unique, and we are committed to creating an inclusive and supportive environment where all can thrive. The Islamic University of Maldives offers a world of opportunities, and we encourage you to make the most of them.


Explore our services, reach out to us with your questions, and be an active participant in your educational journey. Your success is our shared goal, and we are excited to be part of this incredible chapter in your life.


Wishing you a rewarding and enriching academic adventure.

Overview

The Department of Student Affairs at the Islamic University of Maldives is dedicated to enriching the student experience. We provide essential services including academic support, counseling, and career guidance, creating a supportive environment for academic and personal growth. Our commitment is to empower students to thrive, engage in campus life, and prepare for a successful future.


For further details and assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to our dedicated team. We're here to ensure that your time at our university is a rewarding and memorable journey.

Vision

To be the catalyst of student success, fostering a vibrant and inclusive university community where every student realizes their full potential and becomes an active, responsible, and engaged global citizen.

Mission

We are dedicated to providing comprehensive student services and support. Our mission is to empower students to excel academically, grow personally, and contribute positively to the university community and beyond. We strive to create an environment where diversity is celebrated, inclusivity is embraced, and every student's unique journey is valued.

Values and Ethics

  1. Student-Centered: We put students' needs and well-being at the forefront of our services and decisions.
  2. Inclusivity: We embrace diversity and create an environment where every student feels welcome and valued.
  3. Excellence: We are committed to delivering the highest quality services and support to help students succeed.
